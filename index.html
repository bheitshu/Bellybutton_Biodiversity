Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Many bacterial species are not studied and many more remain unknown to science. I am conducting a study on bacterial species that have the ability to synthesize proteins to taste like beef. We have partnered with a Synthetic Beef company to research species to synthesis meat. So far success has come from algae, fungi and microorganisms found on plant roots. However, the search is still on for the perfect bacteria to provide the taste of beef. We believe the best micro-organism is found right on our very own body, especially in the bellybutton. We have tested the different bacteria in navels across the country to find the perfect bacteria to create synthetic beef.
